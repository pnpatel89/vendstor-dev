What is Vendstor?

Vendstor is an online marketplace for wholesale distributors and their clients. Vendstor allows distributors to catalog their inventory online and make it available for purchase to approved clients.

Why should I use Vendstor?

For distributors:

Vendstor's easy to use interface makes it simple for clients to view and search for products. Additionally, Vendstor enables direct payment with a flat fee, which means no longer having to collect physical payments via check or cash. Our cloud-based infrastructure saves costs by letting distributors outsource site maintenance to Vendstor, allowing distributors to focus more on core business and less on technical issues. Distributors can connect to new clients through Vendstor's search platform that lets buyers find who's selling what items.

For buyers:

Vendstor lets buyers easily search for products and create order lists. This lets businesses buy what they want, when they want without worrying about errors. Businesses can also manage separate branches and make separate orders for each branch. Lastly Vendstor offers businesses the convenience of paying directly online.

What if I am a distributor and already have online ordering?

Vendstor goes beyond simply facilitating online orders. Payment is made simple through our support of bank-to-bank transactions online. Our data engine lets distributors analyze sales trends across individual products, product categories, and customers. The user-friendly interface lets distributors add new products quickly.

What will Vendstor cost me if I'm selling?

Listing products on Vendstor will cost a monthly fee that will vary reasonably with database needs. Additionally a flat chart of $1.00 for bank-to-bank order processing.

What will Vendstor cost me if I'm buying?

There are no costs to buyers!

What if my business operates through credit or receivables?

Credit and receivables are no problem at all with Vendstor as distributors have the ability to defer payment processing.

Who can see a distributor's products and prices?

Distributors can restrict price information and even product listing to clients.

How can a distributor upload its inventory?

There are several ways to upload inventory. The easiest way is to send us a file, or even a physical list, and we'll upload it for you! You information will remain confidential.Vendstor also enables you to manually enter your product listing.

What if my business has multiple branches?

Not a problem! Businesses can easily manage purchases for various branches.

How does Vendstor's data engine work?

Vendstor's data engine access aggregate and individual product sales information to identify product trends. Distributors can identify in real-time, both best-selling and worst-selling products in addition to customer purchase habits.

How can I sign up?

Register here or contact us at info@vendstor.com
